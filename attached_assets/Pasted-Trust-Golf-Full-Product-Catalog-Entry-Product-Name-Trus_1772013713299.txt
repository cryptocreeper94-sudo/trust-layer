Marketing Description (Short)
Trust Golf is a premium mobile-first golf platform featuring 45+ courses, AI-powered swing analysis, USGA handicap tracking, score logging, exclusive tee time deals, and an AI-driven blog — all wrapped in a cinematic, glass-morphism UI. Built for golfers who want more from their app.

Marketing Description (Full)
Trust Golf is the all-in-one golf companion built by DarkWave Studios LLC for golfers who demand more. Browse a curated catalog of 45+ world-class and regional courses — from Augusta National to Nashville's hidden gems — complete with designer info, gallery images, amenities, and contact details. Track every round with hole-by-hole scorecards, automatic USGA Handicap Index calculation, and detailed stats (putts, fairways hit, greens in regulation). Upload a photo or video of your swing and get instant AI-powered analysis tailored to your specific club — driver, iron, wedge, or putter — with drills and tips to improve. Discover exclusive deals on tee times and packages. Stay informed with an AI-driven golf blog featuring SEO-optimized articles on tips, equipment, course spotlights, fitness, and strategy. Partners and vendors can apply directly through the platform. Installable as a PWA on any device for a native-like experience. Trust Golf: every course, every swing, every score — one app.
