🌊 Announcement: DarkWave Smart Chain is now DarkWave Trust Layer

We've made a strategic decision to rebrand - and here's why.

"Blockchain" has become an empty buzzword. Every project claims to be a "smart chain" now. It tells you how something works, not what it does for you.

DarkWave Trust Layer describes what we actually provide:
✓ Verified identity through ChronoPass
✓ Guardian-certified security audits  
✓ Transparent, immutable audit trails
✓ Real accountability for real businesses

We're still in beta, still refining the details. But this rebrand reflects our core mission: building infrastructure that restores trust in a trustless world.

The technology is the same. The vision is clearer.

dwsc.io still works and redirects to our new home at dwtl.io.

Questions? Drop them below. We're building this together.

— The DarkWave Team
