TL Driver Connect / Happy Eats

A nationwide, production-ready web application built as dual Progressive Web Apps (PWAs) serving commercial and everyday drivers, food truck vendors, office professionals, and franchise operators. The platform delivers food ordering, parts access, concierge services, mileage and expense tracking, entertainment, marketing tools, and real-time communication — all within a multi-tenant franchise architecture launching April 1, 2026, starting with Kathy's Nashville operation.

Codebase: 183,458 lines of code across 5,084 TypeScript/TSX files, 49 database tables, 192 API endpoints, 59 pages, and 79 UI components.
