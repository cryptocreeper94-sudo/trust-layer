# DarkWave Sprint Rewards - Team Guide

## How It Works

Every sprint worker earns rewards in TWO ways:

1. **BASE** = Complete missions (daily tasks)
2. **BONUS** = Bring people who sign up and buy

---

## BASE REWARDS (Missions)

Complete missions every day. More missions = higher tier.

| Missions Done | Tier You Reach |
|---------------|----------------|
| 100+ | Founders Elite |
| 75+ | Founders |
| 50+ | Core |
| 30+ | Active |
| 15+ | Contributor |

**Mission = Daily task from Admin (share post, retweet, etc.)**

---

## BONUS REWARDS (Referrals)

Share your personal referral link. Earn points when people use it.

**Your referral link is in your dashboard after you sign up.**

| What Happens | Points You Get |
|--------------|----------------|
| Someone signs up with your link | 1 point |
| They buy $5-$24 of Signal | +5 bonus points |
| They buy $25-$49 of Signal | +10 bonus points |
| They buy $50 or more | +20 bonus points |

---

## TO QUALIFY FOR REWARDS

You need BOTH:
- Enough missions completed
- Enough points from referrals

**OR** you can make a personal purchase instead of points.

| Tier | Missions Needed | Points Needed | OR Buy This Much |
|------|-----------------|---------------|------------------|
| Founders Elite | 100 | 50 points | $100 |
| Founders | 75 | 30 points | $50 |
| Core | 50 | 20 points | $25 |
| Active | 30 | 10 points | $10 |
| Contributor | 15 | 5 points | $5 |

---

## WHAT EACH TIER WINS

During presale, you earn **Shells**. At Token Generation Event (TGE), Shells convert to **Signal (SIG)**.

**Conversion: 10 Shells = 1 SIG**

| Tier | Shells Earned | Converts To | Value if SIG = $0.01 |
|------|---------------|-------------|----------------------|
| Founders Elite (Top 10) | 2,000,000 Shells | 200,000 SIG | $2,000 |
| Founders (11-25) | 1,000,000 Shells | 100,000 SIG | $1,000 |
| Core (26-50) | 500,000 Shells | 50,000 SIG | $500 |
| Active (51-100) | 250,000 Shells | 25,000 SIG | $250 |
| Contributor (101-250) | 100,000 Shells | 10,000 SIG | $100 |

**Important:** Value depends on token price at launch. Help grow the project = your rewards are worth more.

---

## EXAMPLES

**Example 1: Good Results**
- 80 missions done
- 15 signups, 4 of them bought $10 each
- Points: 15 + (4 × 5) = 35 points
- Result: Founders tier = 100,000 SIG

**Example 2: Great Results**
- 100 missions done
- 20 signups, 6 bought $50 each
- Points: 20 + (6 × 20) = 140 points
- Result: Founders Elite = 200,000 SIG

**Example 3: Low Conversion**
- 50 missions done
- 10 signups, 0 bought anything
- Points: 10 points
- Result: Does NOT qualify for Core (needs 20 points)
- Falls to: Active tier = 25,000 SIG

## IMPORTANT RULES

1. **No wallet needed to start** - Just sign up at dwsc.io
2. **You earn Shells now** - Shells convert to SIG at TGE (10 Shells = 1 SIG)
3. **Minimum purchase is $5** - Anything less does not count
4. **Results matter** - More conversions = higher tier
5. **Not guaranteed dollar amounts** - Value depends on token price
6. **Work smart** - Target people who will actually buy
